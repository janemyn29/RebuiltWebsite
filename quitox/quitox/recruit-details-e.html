Job Location: .NET/Web Back-End Software Developer

We are a young and enthusiastic team exploring new directions in the industry Marketing. Our leaders are experts in their fields with many years of experience from the world's leading multinational corporations. Is one software developer, you will have the opportunity to join our technology team instead change how businesses will operate in Vietnam.

Job Description

.NET/web back-end software developer, you will:

  • Develop back-end components for cross-platform – web, Android/iOS, using the most advanced tools and methods

  • Work closely with business professionals and software architects to design reliable, secure, and high-performance systems; participate directly in technical decisions

  • Actively participate in the complete project lifecycle, from requirements analysis to final delivery

  • Contribute to knowledge sharing and improvement activities.

Job Requirements

We expect from you:

  • Bachelor's degree or diploma in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering Computing
  • At least one year of experience in developing software for web, back-end systems using Microsoft Technology
  • Practical knowledge of C#, Web API, MVC, Web Assembly, Angular, ReactJS, Node.js;
  • Knowledge of SQL Server development and database design
  • Basic skills in English communication.
